Problem Statement

Fuel injection pump showing calibration drift during performance testing. Fuel delivery rate measured at 185 cc/min vs specification requirement of 200±5 cc/min at rated RPM. This represents 7.5% deviation below minimum acceptable range. Issue affects engine power output, fuel efficiency, and emission compliance. Found in 23 units from production batch FIP-240815 during final testing phase.

Process Flow
